William Wiebe is an artist based in New York. Recent exhibitions include the Museum of the Moving Image, New York; the Singapore Art Museum; the Elizabeth Foundation for the Arts, New York; and NeMe Arts Centre, Cyprus. His work has been supported by the Fulbright Foundation, the Cyprus University of Technology, the National Academy of Sciences, the University of Chicago's Arts, Science & Culture Initiative, Friends With Benefits DAO, and the Foundation for Contemporary Arts, among others. He holds a BA from Wesleyan University and an MFA from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, and he participated in the Whitney Museum's Independent Study Program. He will be a visiting artist at the University of California, Los Angeles in 2024.
