Fixation
Science on Screen: Shorts on Attention, Museum of the Moving Image
University of California, Los Angeles

Hollywood rendering pipelines are increasingly used to create realistic images for commercial computer vision datasets. In Fixation, an imaging program developed by Meta to train eye-tracking algorithms relapses back into human cinema. The truss for synthetic, attention-modulating media emerges here as a film in its own right.

What Comes After Z?
2024

A freelance Ukrainian photographer narrates the movement of images through the circuits of war. As his pictures move into the stock of lighting environments that train computer vision systems, he reflects on the inadequacy of language to the sensuousness of the world. Told through 3D rendered subtitles, his story adopts the training data's own garb.
