灰色市场 (Gray Market)
Exit Strategy, 2019
NeMe Arts Centre, Limassol, Cyprus

Greek-Chinese language textbooks issued by the Confucius Institute, overprinted with images of looted artifacts from the Cesnola Collection of Cypriot Art at The Metropolitan Museum of Art. Counterfeited in Cyprus with funds furnished by the Fulbright U.S. Student Program, priced in CNY with a managed floating exchange rate.
