Temporary Introductions
NeMe Arts Centre & S.N.K. Venus Home Developers Ltd Study Collection
2019

European Council Regulation No. 866/2004 establishes special rules for the areas of Cyprus “temporarily outside the area of freedom, justice and security,” permitting the temporary introduction of goods from Turkish-occupied northern Cyprus into the Republic of Cyprus if they are to be exhibited or used at a public event. Following the procedures enumerated by the regulation, a group of objects collectively exceeding the reporting threshold of €260 were trafficked into the Republic of Cyprus, accompanied by an auction catalogue “providing reasonable evidence” of their intended exhibition.

During the period of their statutory exhibition at the NeMe Arts Centre in Limassol (Republic of Cyprus), the objects were offered for sale for €260.01 each, on the condition of their long-term loan to the S.N.K. Venus Home Developers Ltd (liquidated 2011) Study Collection in Pyla, a bicommunal village within the United Nations buffer zone. Those objects remain subject to confiscation by the Customs Authorities of the Republic of Cyprus today.
