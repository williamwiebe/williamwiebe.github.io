VIZ
Proof of Personhood, 2023
Singapore Art Museum

Support: Singapore Art Museum


The Visual Inspection Zone is the non-machine-readable zone of a passport's identity page. VIZ departs from a series of promotional images of Facebook COO Sheryl Sandberg taken during a “refugee simulation” at the World Economic Forum in Davos in 2010.

In each photograph in the series, Sheryl's identity is combined with the biometric data of passports and identity cards obtained on the dark web, using a custom modification of a software package developed and disseminated by passport counterfeiters. Once a plausible hybrid biometric identity has been synthesized, the image is upsampled using a multi-layered generative adversarial network (GAN). An embedded face recognition model maintains fidelity to the previously-synthesized identity, “verifying” the synthetic identity—designed to pass muster under human and machine scrutiny alike—with the same techniques that transform the image into a photograph suitable for (human) viewing.
