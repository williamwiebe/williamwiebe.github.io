Visual Inspection Zone
Proof of Personhood, 2023
Singapore Art Museum

Support: Singapore Art Museum

Erika, 2023
Photograph


The Visual Inspection Zone is the non-machine-readable portion of a passport's identity page. Visual Inspection Zone departs from a series of promotional images of Facebook COO Sheryl Sandberg taken during a “refugee simulation” at the World Economic Forum in Davos in 2010. From these documentary images of a fictional scenario comes a fictionalized set of images of a documentary scenario: the illicit trafficking of biometric information on the dark web.

In each photograph in the series, Sheryl's identity is combined with the biometric data of passports and identity cards obtained from dark web marketplaces, using a custom modification of a software package developed and disseminated by passport counterfeiters. Moving between the images, the viewer assumes a position proximate to the border officer.
